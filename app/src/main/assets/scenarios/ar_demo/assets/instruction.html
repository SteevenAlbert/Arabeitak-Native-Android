DANGER ① Remove the plastic cover on the dead battery

② Connect the red jumper cable clamp to the red positive(+) terminal of the dead battery

③ Connect the red jumper cable clamp to the red positive(+) terminal of the donor battery

④ Connect the black jumper cable clamp to the black negative(-) terminal of the donor battery

⑤ Connect the black jumper cable clamp to any unpainted metallic part on the donor car

⑥ Start the donor car and let it run for a few minutes then start the dead car.

⑦ Remove the cables in reverse order.
